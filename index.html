WHO ARE WE

AGALI AWAMU DISABLED AND VULNERABLE PERSONS ORGANISATION (ADVPO) Is a community based organization, non-profit, non-discriminative politically,ethenically and religiously that was started in the year 2002 with an aim of promoting better living conditions for people with disabilities, vulnerable and less privilaged. These groups were neglected from development programs, hence the emmergency of this organization as a result of extensive research and analysis that indicated the chronic improverished lifestyle faced by disabled and disadvantaged people

OUR MISSION AND VISION

"Transforming the lives of person's with disability and vulnerable population in the slums into a hopeful population to a better one as well as shedding away stigma that accompanies disability and vulnerability"

"To advocate for social, political and economic reforms in order to improve the livelihoods of persons with all sorts of disability and vulnerability in slum areas"
